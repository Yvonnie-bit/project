Our History


Not too long ago, in a quiet corner of the Milky Way, in a not so quiet planet named Earth was a decidedly unconventional guy named Dave.


Dave was just a regular guy, doing the nine to five grind, minding his own business, doing good, making best his time teaching Science and Maths in a government school... Until one day, he decided he'd had enough. Enough of the straight-laced and routine life.


He wanted to do more. Deep within, his inner "Da Vinci" was longing to break out. He was made for more...He knew not what lay ahead, but was girded with an inner conviction that his calling and time as a teacher was soon coming to end...and a new adventure awaited him.


The next day he quit, laying down his teacher togs, and saying goodbye to crackly, chalky and often red-inked stained fingers. Dressed in berms and his favourite shirts, Dave was finally free to unleash his creativity to the world.



Aided by his wife and muse, it didn't take long to decide that he'd walk the entrepreneurial path by continuing to do what he loved and did best. Tinkering and experimenting , just as he'd taught kids in school. Now though, he exchanged his periodic table, for a growing list of food and ingredients he'd combine and play.... to make the tastiest, most honestly made ice creams to delight young and old...

So he fashioned himself a laboratory in his kitchen and living room, ordered a truckload of liquid nitrogen, and rigged up his own home-made ice-cream maker.
